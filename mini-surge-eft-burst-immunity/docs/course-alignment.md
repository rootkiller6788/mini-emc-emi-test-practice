# Course Alignment - mini-surge-eft-burst-immunity

## Nine-School Curriculum Mapping

### MIT
- **6.630 Electromagnetic Waves** (Prof. Jin Au Kong)
  - Surge propagation on transmission lines
  - Coupling mechanisms (capacitive, inductive, radiative)
  - Impedance matching for surge injection
  - Related: Chapter 4 (Transmission Lines), Chapter 7 (Coupling)

### Stanford
- **EE292: Electromagnetic Compatibility** (Prof. David Su)
  - IEC 61000-4-4/-5 test methods and interpretation
  - Protection device selection for commercial products
  - Multi-stage protection coordination in silicon valley practice
  - Related: EMC immunity testing module

### UC Berkeley
- **EE117: Electromagnetic Fields and Waves**
  - Near-field vs far-field coupling during EFT events
  - Capacitive coupling clamp physics
  - PCB-level EFT coupling mechanisms
  - Related: Chapters on quasi-static fields

### University of Illinois
- **ECE 451: Electromagnetic Fields and Waves**
  - Waveform propagation and distortion in cables
  - Surge impedance of power lines
  - Reflection/transmission at impedance discontinuities
  - Related: Time-domain reflectometry for cable fault location

### University of Michigan
- **EECS 411: Microwave Circuits**
  - High-frequency behavior of protection devices
  - Parasitic inductance in MOV/TVS leads
  - Transmission line resonances in EFT test setups
  - Related: Automotive EMC (Michigan auto industry)

### Georgia Tech
- **ECE 6350: Applied Electromagnetics**
  - EMC test setup design and calibration
  - Coupling clamp characterization
  - Surge generator verification methods
  - Related: Systems engineering approach to EMC

### TU Munich
- **High-Frequency Engineering** (Prof. Eibert)
  - Surge generator design per IEC standards
  - Coupling/decoupling network synthesis
  - EMC compliance testing methodology
  - Related: German VDE standards, EU EMC Directive

### ETH Zurich
- **227-0455: Electromagnetic Waves and EMC**
  - Formal verification of protection coordination
  - Mathematical surge waveform analysis
  - Precision protection device characterization
  - Related: Swiss precision measurement techniques

### Tsinghua University
- **Electromagnetic Compatibility** (Department of EE)
  - GB/T 17626 series (Chinese EMC standards, equivalent to IEC 61000)
  - Surge protection for Chinese power grid (220V/380V)
  - Lightning protection for telecommunications
  - Related: Chinese national EMC certification requirements

## Knowledge Prerequisites

Students should have familiarity with:
1. Basic circuit theory (RLC circuits, time constants)
2. Semiconductor device physics (p-n junctions, avalanche)
3. Electromagnetic field concepts (capacitive/inductive coupling)
4. Fourier analysis (time-frequency duality)
5. IEC standards framework (basic EMC concepts)
