# Course Alignment — Near-Field Probe Diagnostic

## Nine-School Curriculum Mapping

| School | Course | Key Chapters | Module Coverage |
|--------|--------|-------------|-----------------|
| **MIT** | 6.630 EM Waves | Wave propagation, radiation, antennas | L1-L4 (field regions, Maxwell, Poynting) |
| **Stanford** | EE252 Antennas | Near-field measurement, probe calibration | L5 (calibration, NFFFT, spherical expansion) |
| **Berkeley** | EE117 EM | Boundary conditions, Green functions, equivalence | L3-L4 (dyadic GF, equivalence principle) |
| **Illinois** | ECE 451 EM | Transmission lines, waveguides, stratified media | L4 (stratified impedance, Helmholtz) |
| **Michigan** | EECS 511 Microwave | Microstrip, planar antennas, impedance matching | L5-L6 (microstrip standard, line resonance) |
| **Georgia Tech** | ECE 6350 EM | Computational EM, MoM, integral equations | L3 (EFIE, dyadic Green function) |
| **TU Munich** | High-Frequency Eng. | mmWave probing, VNA calibration | L5 (TRL, SOLT, VNA de-embedding) |
| **ETH** | 227-0455 EM | Near-field optics, evanescent waves, PWS | L3 (evanescent spectrum, PWS propagation) |
| **Tsinghua** | EM Fields | Maxwell equations, plane waves, polarization | L1-L4 (foundational EM theory) |
